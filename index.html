managr is for teams or individuals

Managr is a set of tools for teams or individuals who want to keep track of multiple versions of dozens of photos, videos and other assets. Organize your files with tags and folders, easily reference them when sharing with the team, and create previews to streamline the organisation experience.

Simple file sharing for teams

Managr is an intuitive web-based system that enables businesses to stay on top of their assets and files, and organise all information relating to them. Managr divides companies according to departments and sites, and then stores all data relating to each asset or file reference, inside its secure cloud-based environment. From here, users can quickly view current status information, create documents containing relevant data and attachments, facility two-way communication with those working on a particular asset or file, and generate work plans to track progress.

Easy personal asset management

Managr is your digital library and home of all your files: every photo, music file, document, and project. It allows you to efficiently search, manage, organise and share all of the content in your life. It’s the place for everything you want to be accessible – from personal documents to holiday snaps. Once you’ve stored all your files into Managr, they’re just a click away.
